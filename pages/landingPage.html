Assessment Guide

Step-by-Step Guidance on Completing the Assessment

Preparation

Quiet Environment: Ensure you are in a quiet place free from distractions.

Stable Internet: Confirm that you have a stable internet connection to avoid interruptions.

Necessary Tools: Have a calculator, pen, and paper ready if needed.

Technical Setup

Supported Browsers: Use the latest version of Chrome, Firefox, Edge, or Safari.

Camera and Microphone: Ensure your camera and microphone are functioning and enabled in your browser settings.

Starting the Assessment

Login: Log in to your account using your credentials.

Access Assessment: Navigate to the "Assessments" section and select the active assessment.

During the Assessment

Read Instructions Carefully: Before starting each section, read the instructions provided.

Stay in Fullscreen Mode: Remain in fullscreen mode throughout the assessment.

Answer All Questions: Attempt to answer all questions. Skipping questions impact your score.

Time Management: Keep an eye on the timer to manage your time effectively.

Taking a Break

Scheduled Breaks: Breaks are allowed between sections but not during an active section. Ensure you complete the current section before taking a break.

Follow Prompts: Follow any prompts to take a break and return promptly to continue with the next section.

Post-Assessment

Immediate Feedback: Check for any immediate feedback provided after submission.

Next Steps: Follow any further instructions provided by the platform or your recruiter.
